Welcome to Vista Valley! Whether you're here to build a permanent legacy, explore our player-driven economy, or connect with an active, welcoming community, you've come to the right place. 

Our server features unique, hand-crafted systems built entirely with technical stability and long-term world persistence in mind.

### Why Choose Vista Valley?

* ⏳ **2+ Years No Resets:** Forge your legacy on an immersive 1:500 scale Earth map where your builds and progress are safe forever.
* 🛠️ **95% Handmade Systems:** Experience unique takes on survival mechanics built completely from scratch, including our custom Star rankup system for free in-game ranks.
* 🛡️ **Premium Custom Enchantments:** A deeply polished and perfected progression system designed for long-term reward.
* 🕊️ **Safe & Respectful Gameplay:** A toxic-free environment with no town claims warfare and strictly consensual PvP combat.
* 💸 **Player-Driven Economy:** Trade freely, establish player-made shops, and launch global warps in a stable, exploit-free marketplace.
* 🦙 **Capyñata Parties:** Vote to support the community and trigger server-wide events where everyone online hunts for high-tier reward pouches.
* 🔑 **Dynamic Reward Capsules:** Earn custom keys through active gameplay, voting, and daily rewards to test your luck.
* 🎤 **Proximity Voice Chat:** Fully integrated Simple Voice Chat support for natural, immersive in-game communication.
* 🗺️ **Live Web Map:** Track nations, towns, and borders in real time directly from your browser.
* 🙌 **Community-First:** Share your thoughts in our feedback channels and help shape the server's future through active polls.
