Explore our vibrant community and discover what makes Vista Valley unique. Whether you're here to join our server, learn about our community, or seek inspiration, you've come to the right place!

:::tip[What is Vista Valley?]
💸 Player-based and balanced economy.

🗺️ Live world map that is accessible on the go.

🦙 Capyñata parties (hit the piñata for rewards!).

🛡️ Custom enchantments for maximum dopamine.

🛒 Player-made shops for your economic needs.

🌌 Player-made warps to showcase your builds or farms.

🔑 Tons of capsules for you to test your luck.

🎤 Simple Voice Chat integration for easy communication.

🙌 Community-first (have your say with suggestions and polls).

📚 And lastly, a detailed and frequently updated wiki with all the answers you need!
