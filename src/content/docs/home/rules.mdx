## Server Rules

If you have any questions about these rules, please ask a staff member on our [Discord server](https://discord.vistavalley.xyz) before taking any action that may violate them.

:::caution[Account Responsibility]
If you assist someone in breaking any of these rules or purposely ignore them, you will be punished in the same way they were. In the same vein, we will not accept someone else using your account as an appeal reason. Your account is your responsibility, and you must ensure it's secure at all times.
:::

### Use Common Sense

This is the foundational rule. Even if something is not explicitly listed, it may still be enforceable. Staff have the final say, and severe violations may skip warning steps. If unsure about a rule or action, please ask. Honest mistakes are treated leniently, but ignorance is not an excuse.

### No Disrespectful Language, Bullying, or Harassment

Good faith and light-hearted banter are welcome, but cease immediately if someone expresses discomfort, and apologize. This server is for relaxation and enjoyment; disrespectful behavior disrupts everyone's experience and fosters toxicity. Remember, there's a person on the other side of the screen.

Disrespectful language, bullying, or harassment includes, but is not limited to:

* **Hate speech:** This encompasses any language that attacks or demeans a person or group based on their race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, gender, gender identity, disability, or other protected characteristic.
* **Targeted insults and personal attacks:** This includes any language intended to demean, humiliate, or belittle another user.
* **Harassment:** This includes repeated unwelcome contact, stalking, or any other behavior that creates a hostile environment.
* **Threats of violence or harm:** This includes any language that suggests or implies physical harm to another person.
* **Sexual content or harassment:** This includes any language or imagery that is sexually suggestive, exploitative, or abusive.
* **Suicide jokes or encouragement of self-harm:** This includes any language that trivializes or encourages self-destructive behavior.
* **Discrimination based on political or religious beliefs:** While respectful discussion is permitted, any language intended to demean or marginalize someone's beliefs is prohibited.
* **Slurs and derogatory terms:** This includes any language that is intended to be offensive or demeaning, even if used in a "self-referential" manner.

While swearing is allowed in moderation, avoid targeting other players. Severe language warrants harsher penalties.

### No NSFW or Explicit Content

This Minecraft server is accessible to minors. Therefore, any content that is sexually suggestive, graphically violent, or otherwise intended for mature audiences is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to:

* Explicit textual descriptions.
* Sexualized images or videos.
* Graphic depictions of violence or gore.
* Links to external NSFW websites.

While private chat channels may have more relaxed conversations, any content that violates this rule and is reported will be treated as a global incident and subject to moderation. The safety and comfort of all users, especially minors, is our priority.

### No Hacking or Cheating

The use of any hacks, cheats, or unauthorized modifications that provide an unfair advantage over other players is strictly prohibited.

Please refer to the dropdown menus below for detailed information and specific examples.

<details>

<summary>Prohibited Modifications</summary>

The following modifications are strictly prohibited as they provide an unfair advantage or disrupt gameplay:

* **Unverified Modifications:** Any modifications not explicitly approved by server staff.
* **Bug Exploits:** Any use of in-game bugs or glitches to gain an unfair advantage.
* **Nether Roof Exploits:** Any method of accessing or utilizing the area above the Nether roof for purposes other than those explicitly permitted by server staff.
* **Duplication Glitches:** Any method of duplicating items outside of intended in-game mechanics.
* **PvP Advantage Modifications:** Any modifications that provide an unfair advantage in player-versus-player combat, including:
  * Aimbots and killauras.
  * Speed and fly hacks.
* **X-Ray Modifications:** Any modifications or resource packs that allow players to see through blocks to locate hidden resources or structures.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Allowed Modifications</summary>

The following modifications are permitted for use on the server:

* **Minimaps and Waypoint Mods (Surface Only):** Modifications that display surface terrain and waypoints, but do not include cave mode or entity tracking.
* **Recipe Viewer Mods:** Modifications like NotEnoughItems (NEI) or JustEnoughItems (JEI) for viewing crafting recipes and item information.
* **Optimization Mods:** Modifications like OptiFine or Sodium that improve game performance.
* **Chunk Caching Mods:** Modifications like Bobby that improve chunk loading and rendering.
* **Fullbright Mods:** Modifications that increase visibility in dark areas.
* **Schematica/Litematica (Holograms Only):** Modifications used for displaying holograms of building schematics, but not for automated block placement (printer functionality).
* **Cosmetic Resource Packs and Shaders:** Modifications that change the visual appearance of the game without providing gameplay advantages.
* **Personal Stat Displays:** Modifications that display personal player statistics.
* **Inventory Sorter Mods:** Modifications that automatically sort inventory items.
* **Shulker Box Peekers:** Modifications that allow viewing the contents of shulker boxes without opening them.

</details>

:::caution[Automation, AFK, and Grinding]
We have specific rules regarding automation to balance quality-of-life against fairness. These rules depend on whether you are AFK (Away From Keyboard) or present.

##### AFK (Away From Keyboard) Rules
* **Active AFK Automation:** Using any method to automate player actions (attacking, placing blocks, fishing, etc.) while you are AFK is forbidden. This includes auto-clickers, scripts, macros, or taping down a mouse button.
* **Passive AFK:** You are allowed to be AFK to receive benefits that do not require automated player input. This includes standing in the [Glim Hub](/earth/gameplay/worlds/#glim-hub) for passive income or keeping chunks loaded for a redstone farm.
##### Attended (At Your Keyboard) Rules
* To reduce tedious grinding, you may use tools like an auto-clicker for specific tasks **only while you are present at your computer** and able to respond to staff. Some examples include:
  * Repetitive non-combat tasks (e.g., breaking blocks in a generator, clearing terrain).
  * Attacking mobs in a farm or grinder.
:::

### No Griefing or Stealing in Protected Areas

Buildings in unclaimed land on Earth or resource worlds (Resource, Nether, End) are subject to raiding. Use the [Towny](https://wiki.vistavalley.xyz/earth/towny) plugin to claim and secure your land and valuables. You must not attempt to bypass protections or grief towns, including your own.

Intentional destruction near town borders is prohibited and treated as griefing. Creating claims to deliberately hinder a town's expansion, such as encircling or walling off land to restrict their growth, is also forbidden.

### No Unsolicited Advertising

We strictly prohibit unsolicited advertisements, including promoting external servers, websites, or services. Please refrain from any form of spam or self-promotion outside of designated channels or areas within our community. Responsible sharing of information within our community is welcome.

### Multiple Accounts and Cross-Platform Accounts

Alternate accounts from the same IP address are strictly prohibited. Each account must have a unique owner. The use of bot accounts for any purpose is strictly forbidden.

**Cross-Platform Accounts (Java and Bedrock):**

* Java Edition players are permitted to register one (1) alternate Bedrock Edition account for cross-play functionality.
* Information on joining the server via Bedrock Edition can be found [here](https://wiki.vistavalley.xyz/play-now#bedrock-edition).
* Your secondary Bedrock account must not be online while your primary Java account is active, except for the following limited circumstances:
  * Town invites.
  * Item swaps (you must notify a staff member beforehand to avoid suspicion).
* Your secondary Bedrock account must reside within the same town (or nation) as your primary Java account.
* Your secondary Bedrock account cannot manage its own town.

### No Glorification or Excessive Referencing of Harmful Ideologies

The glorification or excessive referencing of historical or contemporary ideologies that promote hatred, discrimination, or violence is strictly prohibited. While respectful and contextually appropriate parodies or light references may be permissible, staff will exercise discretion to ensure they do not create a hostile environment. This rule is intentionally fluid to address the evolving nature of harmful ideologies. While context matters, players should err on the side of caution. The goal is to prevent the normalization of hateful ideas and ensure all players feel comfortable. If you have to question whether a joke or reference crosses the line, it is best to refrain. Staff discretion is final in these matters.

### Keep Public Chat in English or Bahasa

To facilitate effective moderation and ensure clear communication, please restrict public chat to English, Bahasa Melayu, or Bahasa Indonesia. Other languages are permitted in private chat channels.

### Do Not Excessively Ping Others

Respect others' enjoyment by limiting pings to relevant matters only. As a guideline, avoid pinging offline players for non-urgent issues, mentioning users who have asked not to be pinged, or mass-pinging roles for a general question.

### No Scamming

Scamming other players is strictly prohibited. All agreements must be upheld honestly and completely. This includes, but is not limited to:

* Deliberate deception or omission of details.
* Selling renamed items to mislead others about their value.
* Selling mundane items as valuable or rare items. (e.g., selling a candle as a "Capsule Key" for $5,000).

Obvious joke items, like "Holy Water" (a renamed bottle of water sold for $50), are permitted, provided they are clearly presented as a joke.

Valid proof of agreements is required, such as in-game chat screenshots or signed books. Discord screenshots can be used as supplementary evidence but are not considered primary proof due to verification limitations.

### No Sharing Personal Information

Sharing another person's personal information without their explicit consent is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, addresses, passwords, and any other sensitive data.

### Respectful Combat

Player-versus-player (PvP) combat is prohibited unless explicitly agreed upon by all involved parties prior to engagement. Logging out during combat to evade conflict is also prohibited.

**Permitted Combat Scenarios:**

* **Agreed-Upon Duels:** Fights that have been mutually consented to by all participants.
* **Town Outlaw System:** (Discouraged) Combat initiated against players designated as outlaws within a town's jurisdiction, subject to town-specific rules.
* **Declared Wars:** Combat occurring within the context of a formally declared war between towns or nations.

## Server Guidelines

### Earth Preservation Program

We have implemented many rules and systems to increase the longevity of the Earth map, ensuring we do not have to reset it while keeping it fun to play on.

To maintain the Earth's environment, we also limit certain activities above ground. We do not intend to reset the map, so ensuring it's well kept is pivotal for a positive experience for all. Activities that are prohibited include, but are not limited to:

* Creating forest fires.
* Flooding areas.
* Lava casting.
* Large-scale quarrying (within reason, this rule does not apply within your town's borders).

We also regulate some Towny aspects to keep Earth fair and habitable for all. This includes banning "snake-claims" and misuse of outposts.

* **Snake claims** are long, thin claims that usually connect two faraway claims or outline an area. Outlining with claims is allowed sometimes under the assumption it will be filled out within a reasonable amount of time. To ensure fairness, this period is defined as 14 days. Claims that are not substantially developed or filled in within 14 days of their creation may be considered obstructive. Staff will issue a warning, and if no significant progress is made, the claims may be forcibly unclaimed.
* Any land a town owns that is not connected to the main spawn must include an outpost plot. The exception is if there is a close-knit island chain or archipelago, then only one outpost plot or the home block associated with them is needed. Issues like these are taken on a case-by-case basis, so please contact staff if you're unsure about the legality of your outposts.

### Redstone Machine & Automation

This is a very broad subject so we will do our best to cover every type of machine or farm. If you still need further clarification please ask a staff member before constructing your machine, we will try to update this list as we go along.

<details>

<summary>Prohibited Machines and Farms</summary>

* Machines built with the intention of causing lag.
* Machines that are made to bypass vanilla game mechanics, such as bedrock breakers.
* 0-tick farms or duping mechanisms.
* Fast pulsing redstone clocks. Including doubled observers that act like fast clocks.
* Farms that utilise excessive entity cramming.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Allowed Machines and Farms</summary>

* Farms requiring player interaction. This includes farms that are semi-automatic, meaning they require a player to initiate or complete at least one part of the cycle (e.g., planting, harvesting, or resetting the machine). Standard cactus and sugarcane farms are permitted under this rule, as the player performs the initial planting.
* Fully automatic farms for crops that regrow without replanting (e.g., pumpkins, melons, bamboo) are permitted. However, they are often resource-intensive and must strictly adhere to the redstone, piston, and hopper limits outlined in the [Block/Entity Management](#block-entity-management-in-towns) section to prevent lag.
* Mob grinders that use minimal redstone.
* Automatic smelting and item sorting systems.
* *Most* farms that don't use redstone are allowed, provided they also follow the guidelines. These include Iron Golem farms and Villager breeders.
* Flying machines, as long as they have a set end point and don't travel infinitely. They must also have a switch to activate and cannot run continuously.

</details>

:::danger[Laggy Towns]
If staff detect significant lag caused by a town, they will issue a warning to the owner(s), requesting adjustments within **24 hours**. Failure to comply may lead to staff intervention to preserve server performance. Proper management of farms, animal pens, redstone contraptions, and similar setups helps maintain a smoother experience for everyone.
:::
