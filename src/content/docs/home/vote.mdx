## Monthly Top Voter Competition

At the end of each month, the top ten voters on [Minecraft-MP](https://minecraft-mp.com/server-s333194) will receive **500 glims** as a bonus reward. On top of that, the top voter of the month will be awarded an exclusive flair and a **Capyñata Spawn Egg** that starts a **Capyñata Party** upon activation!

If the top voters have the same amount of votes, they will all be equally awarded.

## Capyñata Party

The Capyñata is summoned after `75` votes have been cast in total. Hitting the capyñata when it's around will randomly award the player with **Piñata Pouches**. After the Capyñata has been hunted, all online players will receive a [**Piñata Capsule**](/earth/gameplay/capsules/#piñata-capsule) key as a bonus reward!

:::note
These pouches contain money, experience points, glims, capsule keys, Heart of the Sea, Heavy Core, Elytra, Netherite Scrap, and Netherite Ingot.
For details, Left-click the piñata pouch to preview the rewards and their drop rates.
:::
