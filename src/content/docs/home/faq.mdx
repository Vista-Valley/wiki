In this page, we answer some of the most frequently asked questions about Vista Valley.

## Will I lose items upon death?

No, you will not! In this server, keep inventory and experience are enabled, however death does come at a cost! Some money will be taken away from your personal balance, town, and nation bank.

## Is Voice Chat mod supported?

Yes! Vista Valley supports the [Simple Voice Chat](../miscellaneous/simple_voice_chat) mod for in-game voice communication.

## How do I use Warps?

You can use the [Warps](../miscellaneous/warps) feature to quickly teleport to various locations around the server. Check out the relevant page for more details.

## What are Wraps?

[Wraps](../miscellaneous/wraps) are decorative items that you can use to customize the appearance of your tools and weapons. Visit the Wraps page to learn more about how to obtain and use them.
