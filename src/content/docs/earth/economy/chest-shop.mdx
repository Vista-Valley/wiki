---
title: Chest Shop
---

The **Chest Shop** is a player-run system that allows you to buy and sell items directly through chests. Players can place items in chests for sale, and others can purchase them using in-game money, **Glims**, or **Gems**. It's a simple way for players to create their own shops and trade goods, supporting the player-driven economy without the need for bidding.

## Commands

| Command | Description |
| ------- | ----------- |
| `/chestshop create [buyPrice] [sellPrice]` | Create a new shop in the chest you're looking at. |
| `/chestshop remove` | Remove the shop from the chest you're looking at. |
| `/chestshop open` | Open the shop panel of the chest you're looking at. |

## Customizing Chest Shop

To customize your **Chest Shop**, simply type `/chestshop open` while looking at your chest shop, or Sneak + Right Click the chest to open the shop panel. From there, you can modify various settings, such as the item being sold, quantity, whether you're buying or selling to customers, and even the currency used. This makes it easy to personalize your shop and manage your sales efficiently!
