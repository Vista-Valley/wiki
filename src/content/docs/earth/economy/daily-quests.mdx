---
title: Daily Quests
---

Players can complete **six quests daily** to earn valuable rewards. Completing all quests grants a bonus **Mystic Capsule Key**. Daily quests are divided into two types:

## Core Quest

* **Fixed daily quest** that remains the same every day.
* The only core quest is: **"Vote 5 times"**.

## Rotating Quests

* **Five quests** that change daily, cycling through hundreds of possible tasks.
* These quests vary, offering diverse objectives to keep gameplay engaging.
