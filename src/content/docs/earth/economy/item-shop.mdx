---
title: Item Shop
---

The **Item Shop** is your one-stop destination for essential items to enhance your Minecraft adventure. From building materials to tools and resources, you’ll find everything you need to progress. Use in-game money to purchase items and level up your gameplay! Type `/shop` to access the Item Shop anytime.

## Dynamic Pricing

Item prices rotate automatically twice a week, every **Wednesday** and **Saturday** at **12 AM (server time)**. While the average price of each item is preconfigured, the final price is dynamically adjusted by the server. This system keeps the economy fresh and encourages players to adapt their strategies for gathering and spending!
