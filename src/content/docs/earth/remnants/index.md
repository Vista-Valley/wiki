**Remnants of Gaia** is an upcoming endgame expansion in Vista Valley. Planned features include:

- Two custom maps: **Aethelgard** and **Draknor**.
    - Wild animals and monsters roam these lands; players can hunt them for loot.
    - The maps act as sandboxes, letting players interact with the environment and cause chaos.
- New custom enchantments: **Celestial** and **Umbral**, obtainable through grinding.
- Instance-based dungeons for small groups to team up and overcome challenges.
- World Boss events that gather many players to raid powerful foes.
