## Althea, The Last Seed of Aethelgard

### Background & Lore

To the visitors of the [Glim Hub](/earth/gameplay/worlds/#glim-hub), Althea is known simply as the Sylvan Sage, a mysterious figure who arrives at random intervals. However, her true origin is far more tragic. She was once the High Priestess of Aethelgard, the closest confidant to the Curator of Vitality (Gaia) before the great betrayal.

When the other Curators tricked Raoul into slaying Gaia, the backlash of magical energy wiped out the priesthood—except for Althea. She is referred to as "The Last Seed" because she carries the final, uncorrupted remnants of Gaia's life magic within her.

While Raoul remains in the Void ([The Nexus](/earth/gameplay/worlds/#the-nexus)) consumed by vengeance, Althea travels to the [Glim Hub](/earth/gameplay/worlds/#glim-hub). She serves as a distant moral anchor for the Warden, seeking to aid travelers who might one day have the strength to heal the broken world she left behind.

### Role as a Vendor

Althea is a rare visitor to the [Glim Hub](/earth/gameplay/worlds/#glim-hub). She does not trade for standard currency; she seeks Glims, the crystallized essence of the hub itself.

Her wares are all gifts of the Earth, distinct from the industrial machines of Stasis or the destructive tools of Ruin.

* Unique cosmetic pieces said to be crafted from the memory-laden wood of Aethelgard's lost groves.
* Powerful enchantments drawn from the world's deep magic.

## Orion, The Market Ravager

### Background & Lore

Little is known about the true origins of the being known as Orion. He is a force of nature in the interdimensional economy, a cosmic capitalist who appears wherever chaos creates opportunity. His title comes from his infamous method of operation: enter a new reality, corner the market on its most valuable resources, and sell them back at an astronomical profit.

With the destruction of Gaia, Orion has predictably arrived to capitalize on the conflict. He shows a smug, detached awareness of the cosmic game played by the Curators but expresses no allegiance or fear. He views their war as a lucrative business venture and Raoul's thirst for revenge as a profitable market driver.

### Role as a Vendor

Orion is the master of the black market located within [The Nexus](/earth/gameplay/worlds/#the-nexus). He acts as the gatekeeper to the most dangerous content in the expansion.

He does not trade in standard currency. Instead, he accepts only the rarest materials dropped by the powerful inhabitants of [Aethelgard](/earth/gameplay/worlds/#aethelgard) and [Draknor](/earth/gameplay/worlds/#draknor).

* Dimension Keys required to access [The Null-Forge](/earth/gameplay/worlds/#the-null-forge) raids.
* Forbidden Enchantments that break the standard limits of power.
