## Town Ranks

Within a town, you can be granted various ranks that come with specific permissions. Being granted a rank is a high privilege and you should try your best to fill the role adequately. Below is a list of ranks and the permissions they come with. Ranks can be given to residents with the command `/t rank add [Username] [Rank]`.

1. **Co-Mayor**: Can perform all actions the Mayor can, except:
    * Disband the town.
    * Create, join, or leave a nation.

2. **Assistant**:
    * Can manage town land (claim/unclaim plots).
    * Add or kick residents.
    * Build anywhere in the town.
    * Manage town jails.

3. **Builder**:
    * Can build anywhere in the town, bypassing plot permissions.

4. **Banker**:
    * Can deposit and withdraw money from the town bank.
    * Access the town's financial history.

5. **Guard**:
    * Manage jail plots.
    * Assign and arrest outlaws.

## Town Titles

You can assign customised town titles to members of your settlement. These have no gameplay impact and provide some simple aesthetic and informational flavour.

You can assign these titles via `/t set title [Username] [Title]`. These titles can be given special colours, either via regular Minecraft colour codes (`&[code]`) or a specific hex colour (`<#[hex]>`). Nation titles will override town titles.
