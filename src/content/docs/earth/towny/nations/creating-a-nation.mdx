---
title: Creating a Nation
---

To create your nation, your town is required to have at least **10** residents. 
Once that requirement is met, type `/n new [Nation Name]`. 
Spaces in the name will be replaced with underscores upon submission.
Meanwhile, your town is required to have at least **5** residents to be able to join a nation.

:::caution[Cost & Upkeep]
You should have at least `$125 000` in-game money for establishing the nation, plus an additional `$25 000` to `$100 000` for covering initial upkeep costs. Failure to afford the costs will lead to your nation being bankrupt, and after a certain period of time, lose the nation!
:::
