---
title: Simple Voice Chat
---

Simple Voice Chat adds proximity-based voice communication on this server. You can talk to nearby players in real time; the further away someone is, the quieter they sound.

To use voice chat, you must install the **Simple Voice Chat** mod on your Minecraft client. The server-side plugin is already installed, but the client mod is required to connect.

:::note[Star Requirement]
Basic voice chat (proximity) is available to all players who have the mod installed. The **group chat** feature requires [1 🌟](/earth/gameplay/stars/#star-1).
:::

## Getting the Mod

Download the mod for your client from [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/simple-voice-chat). Make sure to select the version matching your Minecraft client and mod loader (Fabric, Forge, NeoForge, or Quilt).

## First-Time Setup

After installing the mod for the first time, you will receive a chat message when you join a server or singleplayer world informing you that you need to set up the voice chat mod. 
Press the **voice chat button (V)** to open the onboarding guide.

For full instructions see the [official onboarding guide](https://modrepo.de/minecraft/voicechat/wiki/client_setup).

## Player Status Icons

This server displays icons above players to show their voice chat status at a glance:

| Icon | Color | Meaning |
| :---: | :--- | :--- |
| 🔇 | Red | Mod is not installed |
| 🔇 | Gray | Voice chat is disabled |
| 🔈 | Green | Connected and available |
| 🔊 | Green | Currently speaking |

## Troubleshooting

If you are having trouble connecting or being heard, check the following:

* Ensure the mod version matches your Minecraft version.
* Make sure no firewall or antivirus is blocking the voice chat connection.
* For further help, consult the [official Simple Voice Chat troubleshooting guide](https://modrepo.de/minecraft/voicechat/wiki/troubleshooting) or ask in the [Vista Valley Discord](https://discord.vistavalley.xyz).
