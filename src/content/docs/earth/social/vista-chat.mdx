## Chat Keywords

- `[item]` - Displays the item you're currently holding in chat.
- `[money]` - Displays your current money balance in chat.
- `[glims]` - Displays your current glims balance in chat.
- `[gems]` - Displays your current gems balance in chat.
- `[experience]` - Displays your current experience points in chat.
- `[ping]` - Displays your current ping in chat.
- `[playtime]` - Displays your current playtime in chat.
- `[votes]` - Displays your current votes in chat.
- `[voteparty]` - Displays the remaining votes for the vote party in chat.
- `[town]` - Displays your current town in chat.
- `[nation]` - Displays your current nation in chat.

## Chat Channels

* Global chat is the main channel for communication and everyone is able to use and view the messages, accessible with `/gc`.
* Local chat is a proximity-based channel that can only be seen by players within 100-block radius, accessible with `/lc`.
* VIP chat is a private channel for players with paid ranks or discord server boosters, accessible with `/vc`.
* [Town](/earth/towny/towns/creating-a-town/) chat is private and can only be accessed and read by people in the same town as yours, accessible with `/tc`.
* [Nation](/earth/towny/nations/creating-a-nation/) chat is similar to town chat but includes members from every town in your nation, accessible with `/nc`.
* Alliance chat is private and can only be accessed by residents in your nation and your nations allies, accessible with `/ac`.

:::caution[Privacy in Private Channels]
We respect people's privacy on this server, communications done through private channels are largely unmoderated and you are free to discuss more sensitive or controversial topics *provided everyone is comfortable*.

Any reports we receive about discussions done in private on the server will be treated as if they were had in general chat, you must still follow our server rules at all times.
:::
