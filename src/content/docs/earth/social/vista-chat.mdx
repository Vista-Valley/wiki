## Chat Keywords

- `[item]` - Displays the item you're currently holding in chat.
- `[money]` - Displays your current money balance in chat.
- `[glims]` - Displays your current glims balance in chat.
- `[gems]` - Displays your current gems balance in chat.
- `[experience]` - Displays your current experience points in chat.
- `[ping]` - Displays your current ping in chat.
- `[playtime]` - Displays your current playtime in chat.
- `[votes]` - Displays your current votes in chat.
- `[voteparty]` - Displays the remaining votes for the vote party in chat.
- `[town]` - Displays your current town in chat.
- `[nation]` - Displays your current nation in chat.
