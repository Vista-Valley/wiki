**Flairs** are unique suffixes that appear after your name in our server. While there are many ways to earn them, the most common method is by supporting the server directly through our store.

## Contributor Flairs

These flairs recognize individuals who have dedicated their time and skills to improving the server.

* Wiki: Awarded for contributing to the open-source wiki repository.
* Artist: Awarded for creating server art and visual assets.
* Media: Awarded for assisting with marketing and content creation.
    * Streamers: Minimum 2 streams per week (4+ hours total) with 50+ followers and 3-5 average viewers.
    * Video Creators: At least 1 high-quality video every 2 weeks with 100+ subscribers and 50-100 views.
    * Short-Form: 1-2 posts per week on TikTok/Reels/Shorts with 250+ followers and 300+ average views.
    * Requirement: Must have already posted 2-3 pieces of server content before applying. Tag is removed after 30 days of inactivity.
* Techie: Awarded for helping with technical tasks or development.
* Tester: Awarded for identifying bugs and providing detailed reports
