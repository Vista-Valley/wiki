---
title: Marriage
---

:::note[Star Requirement]
Player must be at least **12** 🌟 to access this feature.
:::

The **marriage** system lets players marry each other in Minecraft, providing benefits like teleportation to your partner, gifting, sharing experience points with each other, PvP control, and even a kissing feature! Marriages can only be performed by a *Priest* (Amethyst rankers).

### Commands

| Command | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `/marry` | List all marry commands |
| `/marry <playername> <playername>` | Marries two players (Priest only) |
| `/marry divorce <playername>` | Divorces two players (Priest only) |
| `/marry list` | Shows all married players |
| `/marry tp` | Teleports to your partner |
| `/marry pvpon` | Turns PvP between married players on |
| `/marry pvpoff` | Turns PvP between married players off |
| `/marry kiss` | Kiss your partner |
| `/marry gift` | Gifts the item in your hand to your partner |
