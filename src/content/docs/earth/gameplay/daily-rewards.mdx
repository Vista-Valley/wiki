---
title: Daily Rewards
---

You can open the Daily Rewards menu in-game with `/daily` or `/dailyrewards`.

You may claim up to three daily rewards. Open the menu and select any available reward to claim it.

1. **Resident Reward**: Awards a [**Mystic Capsule**](/earth/gameplay/capsules/#mystic-capsule) key.
2. **Glim Collector Pass**: Awards **400 [Glims](/earth/economy/#glims)**.
3. **Gem Pioneer Pass**: Awards **25 [Gems](/earth/economy/#gems)**.

:::note[Note]
Rewards reset daily at 12 AM (server time).
:::
