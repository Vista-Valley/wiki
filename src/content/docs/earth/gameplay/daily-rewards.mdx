---
title: Daily Rewards
---

You can open the Daily Rewards menu in-game with `/daily` or `/dailyrewards`.

You may claim up to three daily rewards. Open the menu and select any available reward to claim it.

## Resident Reward

Eligible residents may claim one Mystic Capsule key each day.

**Requirements:** Player must be at least [5 🌟](/earth/gameplay/stars/#star-5).

**Daily Reward:** One 🔮 [**Mystic Capsule**](/earth/gameplay/capsules/#mystic-capsule) key.

## Glim Collector Pass

A 30-day membership that grants an immediate Glims credit and allows a daily Glims claim.

**Requirements:** An active Glim Collector Pass subscription. Purchase the pass from the [Vista Store](https://store.vistavalley.xyz); it activates immediately and remains valid for 30 days from the date of purchase.

**Daily Reward:** 800 glims.

**Upfront Bonus:** 6,000 glims credited instantly upon purchase.

**Total Value:** 30,000 glims.

:::caution[Stacking Daily Rewards]
Daily Glims **do not** stack if missed. Be sure to log in and play for at least 30 minutes each day to claim your reward.
:::

## Gem Pioneer Pass

A 30-day membership that grants an immediate Gems credit and allows a daily Gems claim.

**Requirements:** An active Gem Pioneer Pass subscription. Purchase the pass from the [Vista Store](https://store.vistavalley.xyz); it activates immediately and remains valid for 30 days from the date of purchase.

**Daily Reward:** 25 gems.

**Upfront Bonus:** 250 gems credited instantly upon purchase.

**Total Value:** 1,000 gems.

:::caution[Stacking Daily Rewards]
Daily Gems **do not** stack if missed. Be sure to log in each day to claim your reward.
:::

## Booster Pass

A membership that grants an immediate Glims credit and allows a daily Glims claim.

**Requirements:** Player must be boosting the [Vista Valley Discord](https://discord.vistavalley.xyz/) server.

**Daily Reward:** 250 glims.

**Total Value:** ∞ glims.

:::caution[Stacking Daily Rewards]
Daily Glims **do not** stack if missed.
:::

## Culler Pass

A 30-day membership that allows a daily [stack culler](/earth/gameplay/custom-enchantments/#stack-culler) claim.

**Requirements:** An active Culler Pass subscription. Purchase the pass from the [Vista Store](https://store.vistavalley.xyz); it activates immediately and remains valid for 30 days from the date of purchase.

**Daily Reward:** 45 minutes of [stack culler](/earth/gameplay/custom-enchantments/#stack-culler).

:::caution[Stacking Daily Rewards]
Daily [stack culler](/earth/gameplay/custom-enchantments/#stack-culler) **do not** stack if missed. Be sure to log in each day to claim your reward.
:::

---

:::note[Note]
Rewards reset daily at 12 AM (server time).
:::
