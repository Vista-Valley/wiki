## What is a Mortal Echo?

When you die on the server, your items and experience points are not dropped on the ground. Instead, they are safely stored in a **Mortal Echo**, a swirling wisp of your soul's energy that appears at your death location.

To collect your belongings, simply **walk through** the particle effect. There's no need to right-click or break anything. This system is designed to protect your hard-earned gear from despawning or being easily stolen, while still creating a challenge to recover it.

## How It Works: The Timer

Every Mortal Echo has a limited lifespan, broken into two phases. You must retrieve your echo before it bursts.

* **Stable Phase** (25 minutes)
  * For the first four minutes, your Mortal Echo is safe and stable. It will gently float, waiting for you to return.
* **Unstable Phase** (5 minutes)
  * After four minutes, the echo becomes unstable. You will see warning animations and hear sounds indicating that it is about to collapse. This is your final chance to retrieve it.
* **The Burst**
  * If the timer runs out, the Mortal Echo permanently fades. All of your items and a portion of your stored experience points are lost forever.

## Key Features

* You recover **100%** of your stored experience upon retrieving your own Mortal Echo.
* You can have a maximum of **5** Mortal Echoes active at one time. If you die a fourth time, your oldest echo immediately vanishes.
* A faint particle trail can sometimes be seen in the air, guiding you back in the direction of your lost echo.
