Custom enchantments allow players to enhance their tools, weapons, and armor with unique abilities beyond the standard enchantments available in the game. These enchantments can provide various benefits, such as increased durability, special attack effects, or improved resource gathering.

## Enchantment Tiers

There are several tiers of custom enchantments, each offering different levels of power and rarity:

Some custom enchantments can inflict special status effects on targets. Below is a list of status effects associated with custom enchantments:

## Abilities

Certain custom enchantments grant special abilities that can be activated under specific conditions. These abilities often have cooldowns and may require specific actions to trigger.
