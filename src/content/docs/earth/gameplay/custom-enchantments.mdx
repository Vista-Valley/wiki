Custom Enchantments allow players to enhance their tools, weapons, and armor with unique abilities beyond the standard enchantments available in the game. These enchantments can provide various benefits, such as increased durability, special attack effects, or improved resource gathering.

---

### 📑 How to Obtain

You may purchase most of the presented Custom Enchantments via the command `/archmage` with the cost of EXP ✨.
Each purchase will be given an Orb based on the tiers you choosed.

:::important[Unlock Requirement]

You are able to access `/archmage` once ranked up to **Star Rank 11 🌟**.  

👉 Check out [Ranks](/earth/gameplay/stars)

:::

Once purchased your desired Custom Enchantments from the `/archmage`, you will be given the Custom Enchantments Orb.
Which will give you random Custom Enchantments of said tiers.
> 🎯 **ACTION**: *Right-click* while holding the orb to view its enchantments and success rate.

### 📑 How to Apply

Be mindful, it all comes with success rates, it **Will** destroy your item if failed. 
Learn how to protect your items from failed enchantments, [Modifications](#modifications)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> Each tools & armors have total Custom Enchantment slots of **5** only.

> 🎯 **ACTION**: Within your inventory, simply *Click / Tap* to grab and *Click / Tap* again onto your desired equipment you wanted to apply.

Here are some items that may assist you to modify and protect your Custom Enchantment books and equipments.
Some of the items requires player to purchase them from **Althea**, located within **Glimhub**. Simply teleport there via `/glimhub`.

---

### 📜 Fortification Scroll

The Fortification Scroll  is an item modifier that prevents an item from breaking when a Custom Enchanting attempt fails. If the enchantment is unsuccessful, the item remains intact, but the scroll is consumed. A new Fortification Scroll must be applied to protect the item again.

> 🎯 **ACTION**: Within your inventory, simply *Click / Tap* to grab and *Click / Tap* again onto your desired equipment you wanted to apply.

*You may obtain it via purchasing from **Althea**, at the cost of `2500 glims point`*

---

### 🗻 Arcane Dust

Arcane Dust is an item modifier to increase the success rate of your Custom Enchantments from **1% up to 5%** per dust depending on said tiers.
Be mindful that **Sealed Dust** may also fail on opening, you will obtain a failed dust called **Mystery Dust** that may have some *mystery* usage.

> 🎯 **ACTION**: Simply *Right-Click* the **Sealed Dust** while holding on your hand to reveal the **Arcane Dust** success percentage of said tiers.

Once obtained with shown dust percentage, you may apply them on to your Custom Enchantment Books to increase the success percentages.

> 🎯 **ACTION**: Within your inventory, simply *Click / Tap* to grab and *Click / Tap* again onto your desired Enchantment Book you wanted to apply.

*You may obtain them via `/tinkerer` by trading any unwanted Custom Enchantment books into **Sealed Dust**.*

:::important[Unlock Requirement]

You are able to access `/tinkerer` once ranked up to **Star Rank 15 🌟**.  

👉 Check out [Ranks](/earth/gameplay/stars)

:::

---

### 📃 Custom Enchantment Randomiser

The Custom Enchantment Randomiser is used to reroll the success rate of a Custom Enchantment book. This can be useful when the current success rate is low and upgrading it with Arcane Dust would be costly. However, the outcome is random and may either increase or decrease the success rate.

> 🎯 **ACTION**: Within your inventory, simply *Click / Tap* to grab and *Click / Tap* again onto your desired Enchantment Book you wanted to apply.

*You may obtain it via purchasing from **Althea**, at the range cost within `50,000 to 625,000 EXP ✨` depending on Custom Enchantment tiers*

---

### 🔌 Extraction Scroll

Extraction Scroll is an item to extract random Custom Enchantment from your equipment and turn it into a book with random success rate.
*You may obtain it via purchasing from **Althea**, at the cost of `1250 glims point`*
> 🎯 **ACTION**: Within your inventory, simply *Click / Tap* to grab and *Click / Tap* again onto your desired equipment you wanted to apply.

---

### 🧿 Slot Expansion Gems

Slot Expansion Gems is an item to increase the limit of your Custom Enchantment slot within your equipment up to 5 Custom Enchantments.
> 🎯 **ACTION**: Within your inventory, simply *Click / Tap* to grab and *Click / Tap* again onto your desired equipment you wanted to apply.

*You may earn it via purchasing from **Althea**, at the cost of `25000 glims point`*

---

### 🗡️ Stack Culler

The Stack Culler grants temporary self-buff that allows the player to instantly kill stacked mobs for 30 minutes. The effect is applied immediately upon purchase.

*You may earn it via purchasing from **Althea**, at the cost of `1250 glims point`*
