Here you will find an overview of the different worlds available on the server.

## Earth

The **Earth** world is an expansive, realistic map based on the real world, scaled down to `1:500`. To put it into perspective, the world border is `73 728 x 36 864`. Players can explore, claim, and develop towns within this vast environment.

:::note
If you have not claimed the land you are building on, your build will be susceptible to griefing, stealing etc. So, please be wary of this.
:::

## Resource Worlds

**Resource worlds** are specifically for gathering resources. These worlds reset monthly.

* The **Overworld** (`10 000 x 10 000`)
* The **Nether** (`10 000 x 10 000`)
* **The End** (`10 000 x 10 000`)

:::caution
Town claiming is not allowed in all resource worlds as they reset every month.
:::

***

## Glim Hub

The **Glim Hub** is where players can socialize and relax while earning **glims** passively every minute! Players can also access the main Glim Shop NPC from here, and purchase unique items from Althea's occasional shop.

## The Nexus

The central hub located in the Void. It serves as the base of operations for Raoul and the **Remnants of Gaia** expansion.

## Remnants of Gaia

The shattered pieces of the ancient world. These act as the server's Adventure Mode. For full details on lore, drops, and bosses, visit the **[Remnants of Gaia](/earth/remnants/)** guide.

### Aethelgard

Once a garden of life, now wild and untamed. The fauna here have gone feral, and the land is being encroached upon by the forces of Ruin.

### Draknor

A scorched continent where the land bleeds magma. Heavily militarized by Goblins and Robotic Scouts.

### The Null-Forge

High-difficulty robotic factories. Gather a team and venture here to face World Bosses.
