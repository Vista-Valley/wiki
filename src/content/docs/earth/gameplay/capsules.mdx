## Basic Capsule

Players can obtain the key for this capsule from [voting](/vote) the server or from other capsules.

## Piñata Capsule

Players can obtain the key for this capsule from hunting the piñata or from other capsules.

## Seasonal Capsule

Players can obtain the key for this capsule by purchasing it with gems in the `/gemshop`. All rewards in this capsule have equal chances.

:::note[Seasonal Cycle]
The Seasonal Capsule rotates its rewards every three months according to the following cycle:
<Steps>
    1. **Spring**: March 1 - May 31
    2. **Summer**: June 1 - August 31
    3. **Fall**: September 1 - November 30
    4. **Winter**: December 1 - February 28 (or 29 in a leap year)
</Steps>
:::

## Spawner Capsule

Players can obtain the key for this capsule by purchasing it with gems in the `/gemshop`. All rewards in this capsule have equal chances.

## Mystic Capsule

Players can obtain the key for this capsule by completing [daily quests](https://wiki.vistavalley.xyz/earth/economy/daily-quests) or from other capsules.

## Elytra Emporium

Players can obtain the key to this capsule by purchasing it from Althea. All rewards in this capsule have equal chances.
