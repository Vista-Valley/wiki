## Seasonal Capsule

Players can obtain the key for this capsule by purchasing it with gems in the `/gemshop`. All rewards in this capsule have equal chances.

:::note[Seasonal Cycle]
The Seasonal Capsule rotates its rewards every three months according to the following cycle:
<Steps>
    1. **Spring**: March 1 - May 31
    2. **Summer**: June 1 - August 31
    3. **Fall**: September 1 - November 30
    4. **Winter**: December 1 - February 28 (or 29 in a leap year)
</Steps>
:::

Rewards in the Seasonal Capsule vary depending on the current season. The table below lists sample items that may appear; the exact set of items is determined by seasonal rotation and each listed item has an equal chance while in-season.

| Reward | Chance |
| :--- | ---: |
| Legendary Enchantment Kit | Equal chance (varies by season) |
| Celestial Enchantment Kit | Equal chance (varies by season) |
| Umbral Enchantment Kit | Equal chance (varies by season) |
| ...Seasonal Cosmetic Wraps | Equal chance (varies by season) |

## Elytra Emporium

Players can obtain the key to this capsule by purchasing it from Althea for **25,000 [Glims](/earth/economy/#glims)**.

Each key opens an Elytra Emporium capsule which contains an Elytra cosmetic; a visual skin or cosmetic for Elytra. These cosmetics are purely cosmetic and do not affect Elytra performance.

All rewards in this capsule have equal chances.

Alternatively, individual Elytra cosmetics can be purchased directly from Althea for **$10,000,000** each, guaranteeing the cosmetic without needing to open capsules.
